# HealthCareBot
A web-based management system is required to gather blood donors and connect them with the needy people who have high requirements for a particular blood type.
A system that predicts the disease at the earliest is very important to avoid any unwanted casualties. This system predicts general and more commonly occurring diseases that when unchecked can turn into fatal ones. 
No to mention, but a system that sets an appointment with the doctor at the touch of your fingertips!
